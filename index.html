About Us

HealthAssist aims to tackle healthcare problems by providing a platform to store and share health records between patients and healthcare service providers.

HealthAssist focuses on giving power to the patient when it comes to their medical and health information by keeping their medical records stored, shared and easily accessed with the click of a button.
